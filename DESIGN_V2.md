# Papa Curt ($PPC) — Premium Design Direction v2

## New Design Philosophy: Luxury Minimalism + Emotional Depth

**Concept**: A high-end, editorial-style website that treats Papa Curt's story with the gravity and sophistication it deserves. Think luxury watchmaker or high-end pharmaceutical brand meets human-first crypto. Every pixel intentional. Every interaction meaningful.

---

## Design System

### Color Palette (Refined)
- **Primary**: Deep navy/charcoal (`#0F172A`) — trust, strength, stability
- **Accent 1**: Warm copper/bronze (`#C77D3A`) — warmth, human connection, hope
- **Accent 2**: Soft cream (`#F5F3F0`) — purity, clarity, medical cleanliness
- **Accent 3**: Deep sage (`#3D5A4F`) — growth, healing, nature
- **Text**: Charcoal (`#1A1A1A`) on light; cream on dark
- **Borders/Dividers**: Subtle warm gray (`#D4CECA`)

**Why this works**: Navy + copper is inherently luxurious (think high-end jewelry). Sage adds healing symbolism. Cream ensures readability and elegance.

### Typography
- **Headlines**: `Crimson Text` (serif, editorial, powerful) — 600/700 weight
- **Subheadings**: `Crimson Text` (serif) — 500 weight
- **Body**: `Sohne` or `Outfit` (modern sans-serif, clean) — 400/500
- **Accents/Numbers**: `IBM Plex Mono` (technical, trustworthy) — 500/600

**Hierarchy**: Serif for storytelling, sans-serif for clarity, mono for data.

### Layout Paradigm
- **Asymmetric, editorial grid** — not centered, not uniform
- **Generous whitespace** — breathing room between sections
- **Staggered content blocks** — text left, image right, then flip
- **Full-width hero** — no container constraints, dramatic impact
- **Subtle dividers** — thin copper lines, not borders

### Visual Language
1. **Large-scale typography** — headlines that dominate the viewport
2. **Minimal decoration** — copper accent lines, subtle geometric shapes
3. **Photography/Illustration** — cinematic, high-contrast, editorial quality
4. **Micro-interactions** — smooth scrolls, fade-in reveals, hover states
5. **Negative space** — emptiness is as important as content

## Section Structure (Completely Reimagined)

### 1. HERO — "The Fight"
- **Full viewport**, no nav overlay
- **Dramatic image**: Father and son moment (warm, intimate, powerful)
- **Minimal text overlay**: Just "$PPC" + "A Movement Born From Love"
- **Single CTA button**: Positioned bottom-right, copper accent
- **Scroll indicator**: Subtle arrow animation

### 2. THE STORY — Narrative Flow
- **Full-width image** (left side, 60% width)
- **Text block** (right side, 40% width, positioned lower)
- **Large pull quote** spanning full width below
- **No cards, no boxes** — pure editorial layout

### 3. MISSION — Three Pillars
- **Horizontal scroll cards** (desktop) or stacked (mobile)
- **Each card**: Icon + title + description
- **Copper accent lines** above each card
- **No background color** — just text + accent

### 4. TOKENOMICS — Data as Art
- **Large, bold numbers** (animated count-up)
- **Minimal labels** below
- **Allocation shown as elegant bars** (copper gradient)
- **No pie charts** — too common

### 5. ROADMAP — Timeline
- **Vertical line** (copper) running down center
- **Alternating left/right content blocks**
- **Dates/phases** in mono font
- **Minimal, elegant, editorial**

### 6. COMMUNITY — Call to Action
- **Large headline** + description
- **Social icons** (minimal, copper)
- **Email signup** (optional, elegant form)
- **Contract address** in mono, copyable

---

## Interaction & Animation

- **Scroll reveals**: Content fades in as user scrolls (no bounce, just fade)
- **Hover states**: Subtle color shift (navy → copper accent)
- **Button interactions**: Smooth scale + shadow on hover
- **Parallax**: Subtle, only on hero image
- **No flashy animations** — everything feels intentional and premium

---

## Key Differentiators from v1

1. **No glassmorphism** — too trendy, not premium
2. **No bright green** — replaced with sophisticated copper + navy
3. **Asymmetric layouts** — not centered grids
4. **Editorial photography** — not generic stock
5. **Serif headlines** — editorial, powerful, different
6. **Minimal UI elements** — buttons, cards, etc. are understated
7. **Luxury color palette** — navy + copper screams high-end
8. **Whitespace as design** — emptiness is intentional
9. **No animations for animation's sake** — every motion has purpose
10. **Premium typography pairing** — Crimson + Sohne is chef's kiss

## Mood & Tone

- **Serious but hopeful**
- **Editorial, not corporate**
- **Luxury, not flashy**
- **Human-first, not tech-first**
- **Timeless, not trendy**

This is a website that looks like it could be in a design magazine. It respects the Papa Curt story and treats it with the gravity it deserves.
